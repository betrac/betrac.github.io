About the Challenge

Bridging the Gap in
End-to-End Audio Reasoning

Large audio language models (LALMs) have made impressive strides on short-context benchmarks, yet long-form audio reasoning remains an open frontier. On Synth-DoPaCo, E2E models show hallucination rates of 99–100% versus 21–23% for cascaded ASR pipelines — despite identical architectures. BeTraC isolates and attacks this gap head-on.

01 ·

Standardized Evaluation

Establish a reproducible, community-wide benchmark for long-form E2E audio reasoning in clinical settings.

02 ·

Quantify the Gap

Precisely measure the performance delta between E2E and cascaded systems under controlled, identical conditions.

03 ·

Stimulate Research

Motivate new E2E model architectures and training strategies that close the hallucination and faithfulness gap.

04 ·

Assess Generalization

Evaluate how synthetic-trained systems transfer to real clinical audio via post-competition out-of-domain evaluation.

Competition Tracks

Two Tracks,
One Hard Rule

Both tracks require open-weight models only and share the same constraint: no intermediate transcription. Systems must reason directly from raw audio to structured SOAP notes.

⚡
Lightweight Track
≤ 3B Parameters
  • ✓ Open-weight models up to 3B parameters
  • ✓ Direct E2E audio-to-SOAP pipeline
  • ✗ No tool use or agentic pipelines
  • ✗ No intermediate transcription at any stage
  • 📌 Baseline: Qwen2.5-Omni-3B
🔬
Heavyweight Track
≤ 30B Parameters
  • ✓ Open-weight models up to 30B parameters
  • ✓ Tool use & agentic architectures allowed
  • ✓ Chain-of-thought agents, RAG pipelines
  • ✗ No intermediate transcription at any stage
  • 📌 Baseline: Best of Qwen2.5-Omni-3B / Qwen3-Omni-30B variants
Reference Topline: A cascaded system (Whisper Large V3 + Qwen3-32B-Thinking) will be reported as an upper-bound reference. Cascaded systems are not eligible for competition ranking.

Dataset

Synth-DoPaCo

All data is built on the Synth-DoPaCo dataset — fully synthetic doctor-patient conversations generated with open-weight, permissively licensed models. Speaker identities are strictly disjoint across splits.

Generation Pipeline

💬 Gemma3-27B-IT — dialogue generation
🔊 Qwen3-TTS-1.7B — speech synthesis
📝 Kimi K2 Thinking — SOAP note production

Audio includes two speakers, natural overlap patterns, 66 ambient sound classes (typing, door knocks…), room reverberation, and Opus codec compression artifacts.

Post-Competition Analysis (Top 5 per Track)

LLM-as-a-Judge

Reference-free scoring across 4 dimensions (1–5): Faithfulness, Coverage, Structure, and Conciseness. Also reports hallucination, omission, and contradiction rates.

Out-of-Domain Generalization

20–30 conversations from Fareez et al. (272 real OSCE respiratory interviews) — tests whether gains on synthetic data transfer to real audio.

Task at a Glance

Input

Output — Structured SOAP Note

SSubjective: chief complaint, HPI, review of systems
OObjective: physical examination findings
AAssessment: clinical impression
PPlan: proposed next steps

Hard Rule

No intermediate transcript or ASR output at any pipeline stage.
